FAQ

What happens if I run out of a menu item?
Proceed to Calendar> Today's Menu> Update QTY> Input 0.
How do I edit my menu/pricing?
In the provider website Manage Meals> select the meal you want to edit>Edit the field you wish to change.
How do I reject/ cancel an order?
You have a small window to cancel your order without charge if cancelled after the order I accepted a cancellation fee will apply. If a provider wants to reject an order a notification of the order will be placed online and you can either accept or reject the order.
How does delivery take place?
If using your own delivery partner you will receive a notification on the drivers app and accept/ If you are using our delivery partner the partner will receive a notification and accept the pick up request.
What happens when an order is placed?
You will receive a notification on the Provider website once a customer has completed a transactions you will then have the option to accept or reject the order if rejected;. if rejected the customer will receive a refund.
When will I get paid?
What is our service fee?
Our service fee is built in from the provider the provider must consider we take a 10% Service Fee based on the total of the meal.
Where is my delivery partner?
Once the delivery partner has accepted the pick up a notification will be sent to you with an ETA.
I handed over the wrong order to delivery partner?
ncorrect orders can either be refunded or replaced depending on what the customer has agreed to.The provider will be liable to replace the meal at no additional cost and a penalty fee will be charged to the provider for the delivery partner
Rate your delivery partner?
You cannot rate the delivery partner as a provider however if Please log a call on the Foodeze info@foodeze.co.za you have any issues that you would like to inform us about you can log a call Operations@foodeze.co.za.
My restaurant is not visible on the customer app?
This could mean that either the admin has not approved your restaurant request or there is a technical error please log a call on Operations@foodeze.co.za.
How do provider payments work?
Providers will be paid between 5-7 days may change due to public holidays
How do I setup my payment details?
On the provider website go to Settings> Banking Details> once you have changed your banking details select the update button and refresh.
Statement or Account queries?
On the provider website select Earnings> Input Invoice date and run the statement you can export to PDF or Excel for your convenience.
Cancelled orders?
Cancelled orders will not be at the expense of the provider, the customer will be liable for a cancellation fee once the provider has accepted the order.
What is a scheduled order?
A scheduled order is based on your calendar meals customers will be able to order in advance in order to make weekly meal planning easier.
I cannot fulfil a catering request?
When a Special request/ Catering request is placed the Provider selected will be notified and will be able to send pricing to the customer. If the provider is unable to meet the requirements they can choose to reject the request.
I forgot my login details?
On the Provider website where you login in click forgot password and follow the instructions you will be sent and email to reset your password.
My device is not accepting orders?
Please log a call on the Foodeze info@foodeze.co.za.
I am receiving orders for items that are not scheduled
Please log a call on the Foodeze info@foodeze.co.za
My notifications are not beeping
Please log a call on the Foodeze info@foodeze.co.za.
How do I view customer feedback/ ratings?
The provider will be able to view the customer feedback on the provider website.
How do I sign up ?
To register you must sign up on Foodeze www.foodeze.co.za Once you have signed up and attached your documentation Foodeze will verify your information and approve your request pending the information given was authentic.
Please log a call on the Foodeze info@foodeze.co.za.
How do you market providers?
Foodeze markets the Provider of the month on our featured page for free. The other featured slots are paid advertising you can select how many days you want the featured spot to run.You can enquire about marketing on info@foodeze.co.za.
I have a different question
You can contact info@foodeze.co.za.
